/remark vAware is an information system for use with mp9
/remark It uses additional information windows (separate from character/player windows) for:
/remark Notifications, Minimap, Chatlog, etc.
/remark It includes a basic status bar (vBar) to help with info at a glance of your mp9.
/remark It also has sound notifications built in, with configurability.
/remark Each of these main features should be configuratble or togglable to your liking.
/remark Additionally, it includes an action package to help keep your mp9 together and coordinated.
/remark (Auto-Portal, Auto-Flight, Slip-Recovery, Comeback, AutoHide)

/alias {fleeback %0} {--;/var {doit} {$0};/enablegroup {ComeBack};flee} {vAware}
/alias {cb} {@CommandToList(whitelist,/window {$ListItem.mms} {/var returntarget $nameplate});@CommandToList(whitelist,/if {"$ListItem"!=$nameplate} {/window {$ListItem.mms} {comeback})}} {vAware}
/remark two Aliases - Fleeback and Comeback. 
/remark default alias - cb tells all in whitelist to comeback to you.
/remark Fleeback gives a command after the flee - such as backstab, recite scroll, whatever you want. 
/remark The comeback just has them scan and return.
/disablegroup {ComeBack}
